About F1 Pulse

Delivering the pulse of Formula 1 to fans around the world

Our Mission

F1 Pulse was created with a simple mission: to provide Formula 1 fans with the most comprehensive, real-time updates and insights into the sport they love.

We believe that following Formula 1 should be as exciting and engaging as the races themselves. That's why we've developed a platform that combines accurate data, beautiful visualizations, and interactive features to enhance your F1 experience.

Whether you're tracking live race updates, exploring detailed driver statistics, or challenging yourself with our Pit Stop Strategy simulator, F1 Pulse aims to deepen your connection to the thrilling world of Formula 1.

How We Started

F1 Pulse began as a passion project in early 2024, when a team of Formula 1 enthusiasts with backgrounds in web development decided to create the F1 platform they always wanted but couldn't find.

Frustrated by scattered information, delayed updates, and clunky interfaces on existing platforms, we set out to build something betterâ€”a one-stop destination that would satisfy both casual viewers and die-hard fans.

After months of development and testing, F1 Pulse was launched just in time for the 2024 season, promising to revolutionize how fans engage with Formula 1.

Key Features

Live Race Tracker

Get real-time lap times, positions, and incident updates during races. Stay informed with our countdown to the next Grand Prix.

Driver & Team Profiles

Detailed statistics and profiles for all F1 drivers and teams, including career wins, points, and current standings.

Interactive Track Maps

Explore circuit layouts with lap records and key overtaking spots. Learn about each track's unique characteristics.

Historical Data

Access past race results, championship winners, and notable records, providing a comprehensive view of F1's rich history.

Championship Standings

Follow the latest driver and constructor championship standings with intuitive visualizations and trend indicators.

Virtual Pit Stop Challenge

Test your strategic skills with our interactive pit stop simulator. Learn about tire choices and timing strategies.

Our Data Sources

F1 Pulse combines data from multiple reliable sources to ensure the most accurate and comprehensive Formula 1 information:

  • Ergast Developer API - Our primary source for race results, driver and constructor standings, and historical data
  • Official F1 Media - For the latest news, announcements, and race previews
  • Team Press Releases - Direct information from F1 teams about their drivers, cars, and strategies

All data is regularly synchronized to provide real-time updates during race weekends and throughout the season.
